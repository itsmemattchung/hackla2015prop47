FAQ

  • Why is this site important?

    This site is important because the state-wide legal aid referral site, CA Law Help http://lawhelpca.org/issues/expungement-and-record-clearing/criminal-record-clearing, does not provide resources specific to Los Angeles County. Therefore, this site aims to fill that void.

  • What are the benefits of Prop 47?

    For individuals who visit this site, they will be able to initiate the reclassification process which makes eligible felony convictions misdemeanors. When a felony becomes a misdemeanor, an individual has a greater likelihood of staying out of jail and re-integrating into his/her community. Additionally, Prop 47 shifts monies that would have been spent on incarceration to health services & education.

  • Why expunge.la?

    Expunge.la guides individuals through the reclassification process as a first step in expungement of criminal records. Additionally, expunge.la provides resources for invididuals who may want to seek expungement whether or not they are eligible for Prop 47.

  • Is any of this legal advice?

    Through this web site deals with very specific legal issues, it deals with them in a model of community education and general responses. Individuals who have questions specific to their circumstances are strongly encouraged to consult an attorney.
